A HIGHLY CUSTOM ToAU SERVER

Eventide XI keeps the challenge, depth, and excitement of FFXI, but removes the worst parts: toxic claim wars, endless camping, and days of grinding with nothing to show for it. We respect your time while preserving the feeling of achievement that makes FFXI unforgettable.

To accomplish that, we’ve reworked core systems to remove frustration, not difficulty:

  • Guaranteed loot systems -- earn rewards through gameplay, not luck
  • Force-pop HNMs -- no more claim drama
  • Improved EXP and party systems for faster, smoother progression
  • Harder fights -- more mechanics, more teamwork, more fun
  • New mechanics and power options to expand player choice
  • Less travel time -- spend more time playing, not running
  • And many many more! -- come find out in the custom changes page

If you love FFXI but want a version that fits your life—without sacrificing challenge or community—Eventide XI is the experience you’ve been waiting for.
