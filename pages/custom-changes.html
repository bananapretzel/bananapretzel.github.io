Custom Changes

This page summarizes server-specific systems and balance changes designed to increase player agency and reduce unfun grinds.

Mastery

Mastery is job-specific and adds ways to increase player power and/or slightly modify how a job plays. Each job has five tasks; each task has a reward. Completing all five marks the job as Mastered (stars appear while on that job).

Availability: Mastery 1–2 available at launch.

Materia

Materia can be socketed to add stats and customize your character. Access is a mix of unlocks, a gil-sink purchase, and world drops.

Availability: Tier 1 at launch.

EXP Changes

  • EXP chains apply to all mobs that grant EXP (not just EM+). Chain bonuses increased.
  • Within ±15 levels of the sync target: 100% EXP.
  • More than 15 levels from sync target:
    • Fighting Easy Prey / Decent Challenge: −75% penalty.
    • Fighting Even Match or higher: −20% penalty.

Slimes/Shiny

When a mob is killed by a group where all players are below the mob’s level, there is a 5% chance it respawns as a Slime or Shiny.

  • Rewards: 1500 bonus EXP, possible bonus gil.
  • Cosmetics: 20% chance to drop a secret cosmetic (varies by mob).

See Patch Notes for the list of zones where these can spawn.

Explorer Moogles (City Fast Travel)

Fast travel is available among the three major cities. No requirements beyond trade items:

  • Bastok: Trade 4× lizard tails
  • San d’Oria: Trade 4× hare meat
  • Windurst: Trade 2× yagudo feather

Dynamis

  • Each participant gets personal EX currency per run (in addition to standard loot pool currency, which is also EX).
  • Lockout: 6 days (weekly).
  • Currency drop rate buffed: ~1% → 6%.

HNM Changes

Tier Keys & Difficulty

  • T1 HNMs are force-popped with a crafted T1 Key (level 70 synth in all crafts) comprising one of each of the 8 geodes.
  • To obtain a T2 key, kill any T1 HNM. This continues up to T4.
  • Each HNM has four difficulty levels. T2–T4 key drop rates scale with difficulty: 25% → 50% → 75% → 100% at max difficulty (Treasure Hunter does not affect these key rates).

HNM Tiers

  • Tier 1: Serket, King Arthro, Roc, Simurgh, Capricious Cassie
  • Tier 2: Behemoth, Adamantoise, Fafnir
  • Tier 3: King Behemoth, Aspidochelone, Nidhogg, Cerberus, Hydra, Khimaira
  • Tier 4: Tiamat, Vrtra, Jormungand

NM Stat Scaling

Notorious Monsters have increased difficulty. They gain stats equal to their spawn level (e.g., if Valkurm Emperor spawns at lvl 30, it gains +30 to each of the following):

Defense, Evasion, Attack, Ranged Attack, Accuracy, Ranged Accuracy, Magic Accuracy, Magic Evasion.

HNM Currency

Each tier awards a custom currency. After a kill, players earn currency for that tier based on difficulty:

  • Difficulty 1 → +1
  • Difficulty 2 → +2
  • Difficulty 3 → +3
  • Difficulty 4 → +4

Outposts

Outpost warps are obtainable by all nations so long as the outpost is owned by any nation. No more being gated by national ownership.

Limit Break Timeline

  • [LB1] Start at level 45
  • [LB2] Start at level 50 (if LB1 completed)
  • [LB3] Start at level 60 (if LB2 completed)
  • [LB4] Start at level 60 (if LB3 completed)
  • [LB5] Start at level 60 (if LB4 completed)

Crafting: Tier 1 HNM Key Synths

Each T1 key is a level 70 craft requiring all eight geodes plus one crystal based on the craft:

  • Cooking — Dark
  • Leathercraft — Lightning
  • Clothcraft — Earth
  • Bonecraft — Water
  • Smithing — Ice
  • Goldsmithing — Fire
  • Alchemy — Light
  • Woodworking — Wind

Empyrean Weapon

Empyreans are obtainable and will be level 75 weapons.
